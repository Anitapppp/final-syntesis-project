    Linear regression models provide a mathematical formula for interpretation to generate predictions. Linear regression is an established technique and is easily applied to software and computing.It attempts to plot a linear graph between two variables, as in this case the variable X represents months and the variable Y represents kWh. As can be seen in the graph, the linear regression is descending on the Y axis with respect to the passage of the months located on the X axis, since observing the equation it can be seen that the slope is negative 2.00, so that when X increases by 1, Y decreases by 2.00 and the Y-intercept is 126.80. On the other hand, the red points represent the kWh consumption of the household in each month, which allows us to determine that in comparison from the first to the second month, consumption increased, but for the third month it remained stable with respect to the previous month, while for the fourth and fifth month it decreased significantly, analyzing these data I could determine that these changes arise seasonally, since consumption was higher during the vacation season when more time was spent at home, including the consumption of kWh for the lighting of Christmas lights.
